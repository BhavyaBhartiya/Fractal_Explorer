Keyboard Controls

1. Pan :

·W / w - Move up by roughly 20 points
·S / S - Move down by roughly 20 points
·A / a - Move left by roughly 20 points
·d - Move right by roughly 20 points
·Shift W / w - Move up by roughly 10 points
·Shift S / S - Move down by roughly 10 points
·Shift A / a - Move left by roughly 10 points
·Shift D / d - Move right by roughly 10 points
·Alt W / w - Move up by roughly 5 points
·Alt S / S - Move down by roughly 5 points
·Alt A / a - Move left by roughly 5 points
·D - Move right by roughly 5 points

2. Changing Constant :

· ↑ - Increase Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.01
· ↓ - Decrease Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.01
· → - Increase Real Part of Constant by 0.01
· ← - Decrease Real Part of Constant by 0.01
· Shift ↑ - Increase Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.001
· Shift ↓ - Decrease Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.001
· Shift → - Increase Real Part of Constant by 0.001
· Shift ← - Decrease Real Part of Constant by 0.001
· Ctrl ↑ - Increase Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.0001
· Ctrl ↓ - Decrease Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.0001
· Ctrl → - Increase Real Part of Constant by 0.0001
· Ctrl ← - Decrease Real Part of Constant by 0.0001

3. Zoom :

· = / + - Double the zoom
· − - half the zoom

Mouse Controls

1. Pan :

Drag the mouse around to pan

2. Changing the constant :

Before clicking generate you can view the julia sets at different consatnts by moving the pointer around in the canvas to change the constant

Inputs in the Form

1. Real Constant Field

In this field you can input the real part of the constant upto 4 decimals. More than that will lead to it being rounded off. If it is left blank then it will hava a default value of +0.28.

2. Imaginary Constant Field

In this field you can input the imaginary part of the constant upto 4 decimals. More than that will lead to it being rounded off. If it is left blank then it will hava a default value of +0.01.

3. Maximum Iterations Field

In this field you can input the maximum number of iterations to be performed. It should be an integer. If no value is entered then it is taken to be 256.

4. Maximum Iterations Field

Using the drop down menu you can select one of the 4 colouring chemes for the julia set. For higher iterations it is not recommended to use the GREYSCALE them as it won't display anything due the colours being dim and merging into the black background of the canvas.

5. Generate Button

Click this button to generate the julia set for the values that were filled in the form fields

5. Zoom Buttons

Click the plus or minus button to zoom in or zoom out respectively
