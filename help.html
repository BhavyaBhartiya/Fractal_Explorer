Keyboard Controls

1. Pan :

·W / w - Move up by roughly 20 points
·S / S - Move down by roughly 20 points
·A / a - Move left by roughly 20 points
·d - Move right by roughly 20 points
·Shift W / w - Move up by roughly 10 points
·Shift S / S - Move down by roughly 10 points
·Shift A / a - Move left by roughly 10 points
·Shift D / d - Move right by roughly 10 points
·Alt W / w - Move up by roughly 5 points
·Alt S / S - Move down by roughly 5 points
·Alt A / a - Move left by roughly 5 points
·D - Move right by roughly 5 points

2. Changing Constant :

· ↑ - Increase Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.01
· ↓ - Decrease Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.01
· → - Increase Real Part of Constant by 0.01
· ← - Decrease Real Part of Constant by 0.01
· Shift ↑ - Increase Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.001
· Shift ↓ - Decrease Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.001
· Shift → - Increase Real Part of Constant by 0.001
· Shift ← - Decrease Real Part of Constant by 0.001
· Ctrl ↑ - Increase Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.0001
· Ctrl ↓ - Decrease Imaginary Part of Constant by 0.0001
· Ctrl → - Increase Real Part of Constant by 0.0001
· Ctrl ← - Decrease Real Part of Constant by 0.0001

3. Zoom :

· = / + - Double the zoom
· − - half the zoom
